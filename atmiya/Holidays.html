Holiday Information

Holidays will be applicable as per its type as per following details.

H - Holiday for all students and staff members at the University.

H/OH-NW - Holiday / Optional Holiday falling on non-working day at the University i.e. 3rd Saturday or Sunday.

OH - Regular working day for students and staff members. Based on religious belief / need student may be given an optional holiday and accordingly the required academic support to be extended. No examination to be arranged. These holidays are adjusted against the special holidays declared by University as per regional (e.g. Janmastami) or University needs.
